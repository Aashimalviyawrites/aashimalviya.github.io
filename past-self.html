For the Girl I Used to Be

November 2025

To the girl I used to be— thank you for the storms you survived, for the battles you fought alone. I cannot explain how grateful I am to the one who built me— every choice you made, every strength you gathered. For every road you wandered, every version you became, I thank you. I have something for you now— a hug you always deserved for the days anger filled your chest, for the nights you cried in silence. I want to be what you never had: a voice that listens, a presence that stays.
