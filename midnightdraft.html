Midnight Draft

September 2025

A quiet midnight— I sat at my desk. A blank page stared at me, while my mind raced with thoughts. I tried to write a sentence I believed— what fascinated me, what I felt. Yet strangely, I failed. Pen in hand, I stood there, and despite the effort the words never came. With hundreds of memories and a thousand thoughts, I struggled to frame a single sentence. It was not that I could not write— only that my thoughts ran faster than my pen. How could I note every line I felt? That night, my friend, I realised— it is not how much we feel, but how much we can express. So I wrote what I could: messy lines, unclear paragraphs. God knows whether they will ever make sense. Perhaps one day I will edit them into life, hoping they reach someone.
